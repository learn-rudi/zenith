What This Looks Like

A grounded view of AI-integrated wealth management — not science fiction, just your Monday morning made faster.

RUDI — Responsible Use of Digital Intelligence | Prepared for Zenith Wealth Partners

Nobody's workflow changes. Nobody learns to code. The data you already have — across WealthBox, RightCapital, YourStake, Morningstar, Drive, Notion, Slack — just starts working together. Here's what that looks like in practice.

Workflow Transformations
01

Client Onboarding

Today

Prospect fills out YourStake questionnaire. Advisor manually reviews results, drafts investment policy statement language, screens the fund universe, builds a proposed portfolio, and creates a presentation for the first meeting.

With AI Integration

Prospect fills out the questionnaire. Before the first meeting, an agent has drafted the IPS language, screened funds against their values, built a proposed portfolio with Morningstar quantitative scores, and generated a one-page summary. Advisor reviews, edits, and walks in prepared.

02

Quarterly Reporting

Today

Andrew manually pulls Morningstar data and YourStake impact scores into Claude to build a scorecard for each portfolio. Flags funds below threshold by hand. Drafts client-facing impact reports individually.

With AI Integration

Agent runs quarterly — pulls both data sources, generates the scorecard, flags funds below threshold, drafts swap recommendations, and produces the client-facing impact report. Andrew approves instead of builds.

03

RFP Response

Today

85-page RFP arrives. Andrew works through it with Claude, starting mostly from scratch each time — re-establishing the firm's voice, compliance language, and track record with every new document.

With AI Integration

An agent trained on every RFP Zenith has ever submitted — in their voice, with their compliance language and track record — drafts 80% of it. Andrew edits the 20% that matters.

04

Portfolio Intelligence

Today

Chelsea wants to know what happens if a client shifts allocation — requires manual analysis across YourStake, Morningstar, and RightCapital. Multiple tabs, multiple exports, manual comparison.

With AI Integration

Chelsea asks: "What happens if the Morrison Foundation shifts 20% into climate solutions without dropping below a 7 on quantitative score?" Gets a real answer — the agent knows the portfolio, the fund universe, the values, and the constraints.

05

New Hire Onboarding

Today

Nina starts. Months of learning how Zenith operates — processes, compliance requirements, client preferences scattered across Notion, Drive, and Slack. Institutional knowledge lives in people's heads.

With AI Integration

Nina talks to an internal agent that knows every process, every compliance requirement, every client preference that's been documented. Productive in days, not months.

06

Marketing & Research

Today

Marketing uses Perplexity for research and Canva for design — separately. Content creation is manual, reactive, and disconnected from the firm's actual client data and investment themes.

With AI Integration

Weekly automated brief: what happened in ESG and impact investing this week that's relevant to Zenith's specific client base. Draft social posts attached. Content grounded in real portfolio themes.

The Foundation

None of this requires new hires, new departments, or new expertise. It requires structured data and purpose-built tools — which is exactly what Phases I through III produce.
