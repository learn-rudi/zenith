AI Adoption Roadmap

RUDI — Responsible Use of Digital Intelligence | A framework for principled, phased AI integration

Adoption Architecture
Phase I

Assess

  • AI literacy measurement
  • Organizational readiness
  • Risk & opportunity mapping
Phase II

Map

  • Tool & system audit
  • Friction & time-loss analysis
  • Use case prioritization
Phase III

Build

  • Internal tooling & agents
  • LLM process integration
  • Workflow automation
Phase IV

Train

  • Role-based enablement
  • Adoption playbooks
  • Continuous optimization
Governing Principle

Each phase is sequentially gated. You cannot automate what has not been mapped. You cannot train on tools that have not been built. The sequence is the strategy.
